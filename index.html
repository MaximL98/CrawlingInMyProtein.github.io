Project: My Protein data retrieval

My Protein Crawler

Welcome to our project. This is a website created for the Information Retrieval (61961) course. The main objective was to create a web crawler designed to traverse the MyProtein.com website and extract relevant information, based on interesting queries which are not currently present on that website. You are welcome to explore and learn more about us by clicking on our names in the navbar above. The tab "Project: My Protein data retrieval" will take you to the project overview and explanations.
